Stylish, modern and fully furnished apartment with smart home technology, perfectly situated in Friedrichshain. It is located in the vibrant Samariterviertel in the second back house.

Although living in a busy entertaining part of Berlin, there is absolutely no noise in your apartment. Further, directly next to your flat there is a nice and cosy inner courtyard where you can read or enjoy the sun.

The apartment leaves nothing to be desired, so you do not have to worry about anything after a long exhausting trip. From the first coffee or tea of the day to your favourite Netflix show in the evening everything is offered. You can control the light, temperature and humidity of the apartment with your phone as it is equipped with smart home technology. The bathroom comes with a big walk-in shower with a rain shower option and floor heating.

An opportunity to make this beautiful place your home!

Our studio flats for up to 2 people (extra person/child will be charged 150 €/month) are located in the side wing of a modernised old building from 1898 with a view of the beautifully landscaped inner courtyard. Lift is available. Very quiet location, bright and very nicely furnished.

The studios consist of comfy sleeping area with a King size bed (180 x 200 cm) or partly queen size bed (160 x 200 cm), and a fully equipped kitchen with refrigerator, coffee maker, toaster, kettle, cooker and dishes is of course available. The bathroom are either with shower ( H11) or with bathtub (H41 & H31). All available to create a beautiful housing space!

Located on Oderberger Strasse in the popular district of Prenzlauer Berg.
